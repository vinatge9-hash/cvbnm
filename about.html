About Niyantri Cafe

Niyantri Cafe is a homegrown coffee spot in Hyderabad, born out of a love for coffee, community, and calm mornings. We source beans from local roasteries and craft beverages with care to create a warm, inviting space where conversations flow as smoothly as our espresso.

Our cafe embraces a modern, warm aesthetic with glassy surfaces, soft lighting, and a frosted glass look that lets you feel the warmth without sacrificing clarity. We welcome everyone to relax, work, or meet friends, all while enjoying ethically sourced, thoughtfully prepared drinks.
